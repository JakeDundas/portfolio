About Me

Hello! I'm Jake Dundas, a versatile software engineer with a specialization in web development and data science. I'm a recent graduate of Griffith University, where I was honored with the Griffith Award for Academic Excellence in 2023.

Throughout my academic journey, I've worked on several noteworthy projects that demonstrate my abilities and passion for software engineering. My two key projects include a real-time chat system developed using Node.js, Angular, and sockets. This complex system supported various user roles and permissions, allowing users to communicate in groups and channels, with chat history stored in MongoDB. I also spearheaded the development of a Laravel-based food ordering platform, equipped with functionalities like user registration, dish listing, order placement, and a state-of-the-art recommendation system.

While I enjoy the technical rigor of my work, I also firmly believe in the power of curiosity and continuous learning, which are the driving forces behind my career goals. I pride myself on my attention to detail, performance and efficiency, adaptability, and strong communication skills, which I believe are vital for successful collaboration in any team.

When I'm not crafting code or exploring new tech trends, I'm likely at the gym staying active or engrossed in a challenging board game. Just like in my professional life, I'm always looking for ways to learn, grow, and enhance my game, both on the fitness floor and the game board.

As I embark on my professional journey, I look forward to opportunities where I can apply my skills, learn new ones, and make a positive impact through my work.
